Patient & Caregiver Wishes
Patient
🕒Live as long as possible
Some patients want to extend their life as much as possible, even if treatments are tough.
🏡Stay at home
Many patients wish to remain at home, surrounded by familiar faces and things.
😌Comfortable and pain-free
Patients often wish for comfort and relief from pain, especially in later stages.
👨‍👩‍👧‍👦Be with family and friends
Patients may wish to spend quality time with loved ones.
🙏Spiritual or religious rituals
Patients may have spiritual wishes or want certain rituals performed.
Caregiver
🖐️Minimise Patient Suffering
Caregivers often want to reduce the patient’s suffering, even at the cost of life extension.
🧑‍⚕️Comfortable and pain-free
Caregivers prioritize comfort and pain relief for the patient.
🎗️Respect patient’s wishes
Caregivers may want to honor the patient’s preferences, even if they differ.
🏡Stay at home
Some caregivers wish to keep the patient at home, if possible.
🤝Support family and friends
Caregivers may wish for support for themselves and other family members.
